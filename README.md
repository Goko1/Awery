## 🌟 About Awery

Awery is your go-to mobile app for a personalized anime experience. From seamless integration with popular anime libraries to comprehensive customization options, Awery brings the best of anime to your fingertips.

## 🚀 Features

<p align="center">
   <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MrBoomDeveloper/Awery/master/docs/screenshot1.jpg" alt="Screenshot" width="400px">
</p>

### 🎥 **Integration**
- **Aniyomi Extensions**: Use any Aniyomi extension to access a vast library of anime content.

### 🔄 **Syncing**
- **Progress Syncing**: Sync your watch progress with platforms like AniList, MyAnimeList, and Shikimori.

### 🗨️ **Community Features**
- **Comments**: Engage with other anime fans through comments and discussions.

### 🎛️ **Customization**
- **Tag Filtering**: Filter out unwanted tags (e.g., mecha) across the app.
- **Blacklist Unwanted Media**: Keep your watchlist tailored to your preferences.

### 🌐 **Localization**
- **Multilingual Support**: Enjoy the app in your preferred language with extensive translation options.

## 🔍 Planned Features

### 📺 **Platform Expansion**
- **Android TV Support**: Enjoy Awery on a bigger screen with Android TV compatibility.

### 📰 **Enhanced Content**
- **News Reader**: Stay updated with the latest anime news directly within the app.
- **More Extensions Support**: Additional content support through Tachiyomi, Cloudstream, Miru, and LNReader.

### ⚙️ **Advanced Functionality**
- **Torrents Support**: Download anime episodes using torrent support.
- **Offline Playback**: Watch your favorite anime offline by downloading episodes.
- **Update Notifications**: Stay informed about new releases and updates.
